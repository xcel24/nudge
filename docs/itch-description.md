## Description (main body)

**Meet Nudge — the tiny companion that actually gets you to drink water.** 💧

Nudge lives quietly in your Mac's menu bar. Every so often a little mascot walks
across your screen, waves, and asks a simple question: *"Had some water yet?"* Tap
**Yes** and it happily counts it. Not yet? Hit **Snooze** and it'll swing back around
in a bit.

It's not another nagging notification you swipe away without reading. It's a
character you don't want to let down.

### 🐣 What makes Nudge different
- **A mascot for every habit.** Water has a water girl. Vitamins have a doctor.
  Exercise has a gym trainer. Add your own reminders, each with its own character.
- **Build streaks, not guilt.** Every reminder tracks your daily count and streak,
  then goes quiet once you hit your goal for the day.
- **Stays out of your way.** No Dock icon, no window clutter — just a friendly face
  in your menu bar when it's time.
- **Fully customizable.** Set the goal (how many times a day), the interval, the
  prompt, and the mascot for each habit.

### ✨ Comes ready to go
Three reminders are set up out of the box — **Water**, **Multivitamin**, and
**Exercise** — so you can start the moment you open it. Add, edit, or remove any of
them in Settings.

### ⬇️ How to install (please read — 30 seconds)

Nudge is a free, indie **macOS** app, so it isn't code-signed by Apple. Your Mac will
show a **one-time "unverified developer" warning**. That's normal for small apps —
here's how to open it. You only do this once.

1. Open the `.dmg`, drag **Nudge** into **Applications**.
2. **Right-click** Nudge → **Open** → **Open**. *(Right-click is the trick —
   double-click won't show the Open button.)*
3. Find the 💧 in your top-right menu bar. Done!
   *If it says "damaged," open Terminal and run:* `xattr -cr "/Applications/Nudge.app"`
